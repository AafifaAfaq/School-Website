How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to begin your journey at ABC Secondary School:

  1. Complete the online application form, available through the link below.
  2. Submit the necessary documents, including your academic transcripts, identification, and proof of residence.
  3. Attend an interview or an entrance test as part of the assessment process.
  4. Wait for your admission decision, which will be communicated within 2-3 weeks.
Start Your Application

Admission Requirements

Please ensure you have the following documents ready before applying:

  • Completed Application Form: This form can be accessed and filled out online.
  • Birth Certificate or Passport: A government-issued ID is required to verify your identity.
  • Previous School Records: Transcripts or report cards from your previous school.
  • Proof of Address: A recent utility bill or lease agreement showing your current address.

Important Dates

Be sure to mark these important dates in your calendar:

  • Application Deadline: January 31, 2025
  • Entrance Test Dates: February 15-20, 2025
  • Interview Schedule: February 22-25, 2025
  • Final Admission Results: March 1, 2025
