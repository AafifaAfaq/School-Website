Our History

ABC Secondary School was founded in 1990 with the goal of providing quality education to students in our region. Over the years, we have grown from a small neighborhood school to a vibrant community with state-of-the-art facilities and a diverse student body.

Today, we are proud to offer a comprehensive curriculum, extracurricular activities, and a dedicated faculty focused on the holistic development of every child.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and inclusive environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. We aim to inspire a passion for lifelong learning and prepare our students to become responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for innovative teaching, cultural diversity, and a commitment to developing the leaders of tomorrow.
