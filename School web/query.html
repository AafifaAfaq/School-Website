Frequently Asked Questions


General Questions

Q: What are the school hours?

A: Our school operates from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM from Monday to Friday. We also offer after-school activities until 5:00 PM for interested students.

Q: How do I apply for admission?

A: You can apply by visiting our Admission page, where you will find the application process, requirements, and deadlines.

Q: Is there a school uniform policy?

A: Yes, we have a standard uniform policy to promote unity and reduce distractions

Admission & Enrollment

Q: What documents are needed for admission?

A: The required documents include a completed application form, birth certificate, previous school records, and proof of address. Please refer to the Admission Requirements section for full details.

Q: Are there any entrance tests for new students?

A: Yes, all new applicants must take an entrance test to assess their academic level. The test typically covers basic math, reading, and writing skills. Check the Entrance Test section for upcoming test dates.

Fees & Financial Aid

Q: What is the tuition fee structure?

A: Our tuition fees vary by grade level. Please visit the Fee Structure page for a detailed breakdown of the costs, including any additional fees for extracurricular activities.

Q: Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

A: Yes, we offer a variety of scholarships and financial aid options based on merit and need.
